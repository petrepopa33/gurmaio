# Privacy Policy for Gurmaio

**Last Updated**: January 2024

## Introduction

Welcome to Gurmaio ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and share your information.

This Privacy Policy explains:
- What information we collect
- How we use your information
- How we share your information
- Your rights and choices
- How we protect your information

By using Gurmaio, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create a profile or use Gurmaio, you may provide:

- **Account Information**: GitHub username, email address, profile picture (via GitHub OAuth)
- **Dietary Preferences**: Dietary restrictions, allergens, cuisine preferences
- **Physical Information** (optional): Weight, height, age, sex, activity level (for calorie calculations)
- **Budget Information**: Budget amount and period (daily/weekly)
- **Meal Plan Data**: Generated meal plans, saved meal plans, meal ratings
- **Shopping Lists**: Ingredient lists with owned/deleted status

### 1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app (features used, generation frequency)
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, device type
- **Log Data**: IP address, access times, pages viewed, errors encountered

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

- **GitHub OAuth**: We receive your GitHub username, email, and avatar URL when you log in via GitHub
- **AI Services**: We send your dietary preferences and meal requirements to OpenAI GPT-4 to generate meal suggestions (anonymized, no personal identifiers sent)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- **Provide Core Services**: Generate personalized meal plans based on your budget, dietary preferences, and nutritional goals
- **Calculate Nutrition**: Compute calorie, protein, carbohydrate, and fat content of meals
- **Estimate Costs**: Calculate ingredient and meal costs based on average market prices
- **Generate Shopping Lists**: Aggregate ingredients across your meal plan
- **Save Your Data**: Store your profile, meal plans, and preferences for future sessions
- **Improve Our Service**: Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
- **Communicate with You**: Send service-related notifications (if you opt in)
- **Ensure Security**: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### 3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share your information with:

- **OpenAI**: Your dietary preferences, budget, and nutritional goals are sent to OpenAI GPT-4 to generate meal combinations. **No personal identifiers (name, email, GitHub username) are sent to OpenAI.**
- **GitHub**: For authentication purposes via GitHub OAuth
- **Hosting Provider**: Your data is stored securely in our database infrastructure

### 3.2 We Do NOT Share Your Data With:

- ❌ Advertisers or marketing companies
- ❌ Insurance companies
- ❌ Healthcare providers
- ❌ Data brokers
- ❌ Social media platforms (except GitHub for authentication)

### 3.3 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

- **Profile Data**: Stored in secure key-value storage provided by GitHub Spark platform
- **Session Data**: Stored locally in your browser
- **Meal Plans**: Stored in secure key-value storage tied to your GitHub account

### 4.2 How We Protect Your Data

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL
- **Access Controls**: Only authorized systems and personnel have access to user data
- **Authentication**: GitHub OAuth provides secure authentication
- **No Payment Data**: We do not collect or store credit card or payment information

### 4.3 Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: When you delete your account, all your data (profile, meal plans, ratings, shopping lists) is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Download

You can access all your data through the app interface at any time. You can view:
- Your profile settings
- All saved meal plans
- Meal ratings
- Shopping lists

### 5.2 Delete Your Data (Right to Erasure)

You have full control to permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time.

**How to Delete Your Account:**
1. Click "Delete My Data" in the footer of any page
2. Review the deletion confirmation dialog
3. Click "Yes, Delete Everything"
4. Your data will be immediately deleted

**What Gets Deleted:**
- ✓ Your user profile and preferences
- ✓ All saved meal plans (up to 5 plans)
- ✓ Meal ratings and history
- ✓ Shopping lists and item tracking
- ✓ All associated data stored in our system
- ✓ Your authentication session (you will be logged out)

**Technical Details:**
- Deletion is **immediate and irreversible**
- All data is purged from active storage within **seconds**
- Your session is terminated and you are logged out automatically
- Any cached data in your browser is cleared
- No data is retained in backups after **30 days**

**GDPR Compliance:**
This self-service deletion mechanism complies with:
- **GDPR Article 17** (Right to Erasure)
- **CCPA Section 1798.105** (Right to Delete)
- **Apple App Store Guidelines** (Account Deletion Requirements)
- **Google Play Store Requirements** (Data Deletion)

**Note**: This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. If you want to use Gurmaio again, you will need to create a new profile from scratch.

### 5.3 Opt-Out of Data Collection

- **Guest Mode**: You can use Gurmaio without logging in. In guest mode, data is stored only in your browser and is not synced across devices.
- **Logout**: You can log out at any time to stop syncing data to our servers

## 11. Important Disclaimers

### 11.1 Not Medical Advice

Gurmaio provides meal planning suggestions **for informational purposes only**. This is **NOT medical, nutritional, or health advice**. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making significant dietary changes, especially if you have health conditions, allergies, or dietary restrictions.

### 11.2 Estimates Only

All nutrition values and cost estimates are **approximations** based on:
- USDA nutrition databases
- Average grocery prices
- Standard portion sizes

**Actual values may vary** based on brands, stores, preparation methods, and regional pricing.

### 11.3 AI Usage

We use **AI (OpenAI GPT-4)** to suggest meal combinations based on your preferences. However:
- AI does **NOT** calculate nutrition values (deterministic algorithms do)
- AI does **NOT** calculate costs (deterministic algorithms do)
- AI **ONLY** suggests ingredient combinations
